Inter School Science Project Competition
Won First Prize in inter school football competition

I prepared "Heroin's Fountain" for Inter School Science Project Competition.Heron's fountain is a hydraulic machine that demonstrates the principles of hydraulics and pneumatics. Flow of water from high gravitational potential energy to low gravitational potential energy causes a fountain to form, due to increasing pressure on the inside of the system.As the warm air inside cools down, the falling pressure is immediately equalised by the water rising into the straw. The higher pressure outside the bottle forces the water up, which reduces the volume of air inside, which equalises the pressure. But the fountain really gets going when the cold water touches the glass.
